Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What is Smaeka?

Smaeka is an e-commerce platform that offers a wide range of products, from fashion to electronics, at competitive prices. Our goal is to provide a seamless shopping experience.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account on Smaeka is simple. Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start shopping in no time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service through the "Contact Us" page, via email at support@smaeka.com, or by calling our hotline at +21260-7866868.

Orders and Shipping

How do I track my order?

After placing an order, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.

Can I change or cancel my order after it's been placed?

Yes, you can change or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Contact customer service for assistance.

What are the shipping options and delivery times?

We offer standard and express shipping. Delivery times vary based on your location, but typically range from 3-7 business days.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally. Please check our shipping policy for more details on countries we ship to and associated costs.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Items must be in their original condition and packaging to qualify for a return.

How do I return an item?

To return an item, please fill out the return form included in your package and follow the instructions provided.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Can I exchange a product?

Yes, exchanges are possible for certain items. Please contact customer service to arrange an exchange.
